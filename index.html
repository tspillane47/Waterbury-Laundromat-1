🏡 Our story
Waterbury's own

For over 30 years, Waterbury Laundromat has been a cornerstone of this community. Under new management since 2020, we upgraded to 200G-force washers, added card readers to 13+ machines, and commissioned original mural art and a neon sign that make this the most unique laundromat in Vermont.

Serving locals, families, and Stowe skiers — come in, get it done, enjoy the vibe.
